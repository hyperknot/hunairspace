2. Military TMA s AND CTR s (MTMA/MCTR)

On holidays and weekends, and on workdays between 2200 LT - 0800 LT MCTRs are operational only by special request.

Outside the operational hours of MCTRs, civil flights shall operate based on filed flight plans only:

  • Within a circle of 10 KM radius centred at the ARP of Pápa and Kecskemét aerodromes, below 3 000 FT AGL.
  • Within a circle of 5 KM radius entered at the ARP (470722N 0201407E) of Szolnok aerodrome - with the exception of Szolnok city and the area W from the road to Rákóczifalva -, below 2 000 FT AGL.
