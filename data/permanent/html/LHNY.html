LHNY AD 2.21 NOISE ABATEMENT PROVISIONS

Motor planes shall not overfly the town area.

LHNY AD 2.22 FLIGHT PROCEDURE

Motor planes shall fly left hand traffic pattern in case of RWY 36 landing direction and right hand pattern in case of RWY 18 landing direction.
