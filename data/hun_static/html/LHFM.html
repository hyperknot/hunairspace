LHFM AD 2.20 LOCAL REGULATIONS

Start up of engines and ground movements of aircraft may be made with two way radio communication established with the AFIS unit.

Aircraft arriving at, departing from or manoeuvring at the aerodrome for take-off or landing shall use the paved runway.

Grass areas may be used in accordance to prior coordination (special permission) by the aerodrome manager.

Allowed taxi speed on runway is max. 30 km/h, on taxiways max. 15 km/h and at the apron max. 5 km/h.

LHFM AD 2.22 FLIGHT PROCEDURES

  1. Arriving aircraft:

    After landing back track on the runway, movement on taxiway and taxi in the apron for parking may be made in accordance with the information received from AFIS unit.

  2. Departing aircraft:

    Take-off may be made in two ways radio communication with the AFIS unit, according to the weather report and traffic information given by them, on the paved runway 16/34. The use of the grass landing area is subject to prior coordination and permission

LHFM AD 2.23 ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Aircraft movement into or out-off the hangar may be made with still engines only.
