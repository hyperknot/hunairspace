LHPP AD 2.20 LOCAL REGULATIONS

The village of Pogány must be avoided by all engine propelled aircraft, except aircraft making instrument approach for RWY 34.

Local training flights outside opening hours allowed only for contracted partners or on 24 hours prior request.
